All available goats are on our for sale page, wethers starting at $150 each our does and bucks at $400 and up. (Wethers picked up by 14 days of age will be $110 each. At 15 days of age to eight weeks of age, they will be they will be $125 each. At eight weeks of age, they will be $150 each to reflect the increased time and expense in raising them ourselves until weaning.) Our Does and Bucks start at $400. And up.
Goats for sale on this page can be held with a $100 deposit, and must be paid for in full before leaving the farm. Deposits are taken on a first-come first-served basis, and no goats will be held until the deposits has been received. There will be no exceptions. Adult goats can be held at the farm for three weeks while arrangements are made for shipping/pick up, after that, a $3 per day boarding fee will be charged. See the kidding page for details on holding bottle babies.
We can ship goats out of Sacramento International Airport, Oakland International Airport, or San Francisco International Airport. The costs of health papers, transportation, and any blood work are to be paid in full by the buyer. Goats sold as show quality are guaranteed to be free of any disqualifications in the show ring, although we cannot guarantee height on our kids. If the goat and shipping costs have not been paid in full on the day the goat is due to fly out, it will not get on the flight. No exceptions.
We continue to test and promote a healthy herd, free of CAE and CL disease. All testing was negative in 2015 and 2016.
Our quality stock sells quickly and we recommend placing a reservation for the goat or breeding of your choice. We register with ADGA.
Prices quoted are for animals picked up at our farm and paid for by Check (payable to Barbara Tucker), Cash, Money Order or PayPal. If PayPal is used, add 3% to the purchase for the PayPal Fees. Please call us before a PayPal payment is made. Additional charges including kennels, health certificates, state required testing, shipping costs, etc., are the responsibility of the buyer.
Reservations can be made with a $100 (Non-Refundable) deposit for Nigerian Dwarf unborn kids. If the kid of your choice is not born we will either apply it to another kid at your request or you can wait for the next breeding. A $100 (Non-Refundable) deposit will hold the kid of your choice, already born for one month or until weaned, while shipping arrangements are made. If a client changes their mind or cannot pick the goat up for whatever reason, the deposit is Not Refundable and not banked to use toward another sale years later.
If an animal is posted as being "Exposed" to a buck we do not guarantee that animal is pregnant. "Exposed" simply means that animal has been in with a buck for a period of time and should be pregnant. It does not guarantee pregnancy or the #'s of bucks or does. There will be no refund of money under any circumstance for a doe that aborts or the kids pass away.
All Nigerian Dwarf kids are disbudded between 2 & 14 days of age, unless other arrangements are made. Kids are sold at 8 weeks, wormed, their first CD/T vaccination, and disbudded (dehorned), **We have no problem not dehorning if requested in advanced with full payment.
All my goats are tested yearly for CAE and are Negative.
We have the right to retain any of our stock.
